Intro

The 10 Rings NFT Collection is a series of rings each having it's own meaning and power. Made from soul-atom, the 10 rings were forged by the Demon Angels to give power to Tserys, the guard of The Passage. Legend says The Passage was a portal to a world of immortality, wealth, Love and prosperity. Those deemed worthy enough to enter, would never come back. Some say it was by choice, others believe they were captive trying to get back.

Over time, The Passage gained more and stronger enemies from those unwilling to accept their unworthiness. For thousands of years wars were faught and lives were lost trying to force entrance into The Passage. Tserys remained unmatched in power. That is until, The Qwajiti Army was formed.

The Qwajiti Army was made up of warriors weilding powers from beyond this world. It took over 100 years for The Qwajiti Army to defeat Tserys, but in the end access to The Passage was lost forever... Before Tserys accepted defeat, the rings were activated closing The Passage. Tserys' soul was absorbed into the rings and the rings vanished to the furtherst corners of existence.

Since that day, the rings were belived to be lost forever. Some thought they were destroyed. As centuries passed, the search dwindled into nothing more than a mythical story. However, for The Qwajiti Army the search has just begun. Word has spread that they've located the rings and are nearing closer every day. We don't know what they plan do to with the rings. In the wrong hands, the rings can bring unimaginable terror. The rings belong in the hands of someone we can trust. Find the rings before they do.
